About

Hullabaloo was established to fulfil a dream of providing a fun-filled, relaxing and special place for children to receive speech therapy. We have an extensive range of therapy resources and techniques to provide therapy that is both engaging and effective for the children. We also believe that for therapy to be most effective, the involvement of parents and carers is crucial.

Rebecca is a Certified Practising Speech Pathologist who is passionate about helping children overcome their speech problems. She graduated from the University of Sydney in 2012 with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Speech Pathology and was offered a scholarship to study her Master's degree the following year. She has continued to study and stay up to date with the most up to date and relevant treatment techniques and strategies. Rebecca started Hullabaloo Speech Therapy in 2014 to fulfil her vision for creating a space of peacefulness and fun in which to provide therapy for children. Rebecca has many years of experience working with children with and without disabilities. She is also a mother herself!

Can I claim a Medicare rebate for speech pathology?

Speech pathology services are eligible for Medicare rebates under certain Medicare Allied Health Initiatives. For more information please contact us, or visit the Speech Pathology Australia website here.

Do I need a GP referal to book an appointment?

You do not generally need a GP referal to book an appointment, but might to be eligible for Medicare Allied Health Initiatives. Please contact us for more information.

I think my child might have a speech disorder, but am not sure. Can you help?

The best thing for you to do would be to give us a call. We are not interested in providing treatment for those who do not need it, and may be able to put your mind at rest.

Does private health insurance cover speech therapy?

Most private health insurance extras cover includes cover for speech therapy. Check with your provider for the details.
